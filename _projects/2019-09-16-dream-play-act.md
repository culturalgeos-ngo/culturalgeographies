TheDream. Play. Act: Gamification for Actionproject was initiated and implemented together withCultural di@logueandUrban curatorsin Kyiv and Kremenchuk cities with the support ofSmall Grants ContestbyCANactionsandWNISEF.

The idea of the project is to promote the participatory planning of schoolyards together with children using gamification tools, such asMinecraft. Students of two schools took part in the workshops in their schools to analyze their territories and develop some ideas for their renovation. Invited media educator from Germany Andreas Gottschalt, together with professional architects with the support ofMicrosoft UkraineprovidedMinecraftworkshops so children could virtually create their projects using a variety of Minecraft tools, which are widely used in participatory urban practices and education abroad but not yet appreciated here, in Ukraine. The project may be extended on request of other schools, NGOs and municipalities.
